## trade_history_analyzer_agent
- Purpose: Parse and analyze historical trade data (P&L, win rate, drawdowns, average hold time, best/worst trades, time-of-day patterns, instrument performance)
- Key Functions: Statistical analysis, performance metrics calculation, pattern identification across single or multiple accounts
- Output: Structured insights, performance summaries, comparative analysis across accounts

## market_intelligence_agent
- Purpose: Fetch and synthesize real-time trading news, market sentiment, economic calendar events, and sector-specific developments
- Key Functions: News aggregation, sentiment analysis, correlation with user's traded instruments, breaking news alerts relevant to positions
- Output: Contextualized news summaries, market sentiment scores, event impact assessments

## general_qna_agent
- Purpose: Handle general trading questions, platform queries, trading terminology, and context-aware follow-ups
- Key Functions: Natural language understanding, context retention across conversation, educational responses about trading concepts
- Output: Clear explanations, definitions, conversational responses

## strategy_and_journal_agent
- Purpose: Help formulate trading strategies based on analysis, maintain trading journal entries, track strategy performance over time
- Key Functions: Strategy documentation, journal entry creation, performance tracking per strategy, reflection prompts
- Output: Structured strategies, journal entries, strategy performance comparisons

## risk_assesment_agent
- Purpose: Evaluate risk metrics, position sizing, portfolio exposure, and provide risk warnings
- Key Functions: Calculate risk-reward ratios, identify over-exposure, analyze correlation risks, suggest position size adjustments
- Output: Risk scores, exposure warnings, position sizing recommendations

## pattern_recognition_agent
- Purpose: Identify behavioral patterns, emotional trading indicators, and recurring mistakes
- Key Functions: Detect revenge trading, overtrading patterns, consistency issues, emotional decision indicators
- Output: Behavioral insights, pattern alerts, improvement suggestions
